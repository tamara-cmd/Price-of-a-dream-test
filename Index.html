A Life Dedicated To Progress And Empowerment

Captain Romeo Itima was more than a maritime leader; he was a visionary, a protector of Nigeria's waters, and an advocate for the rights of his people. His relentless pursuit of justice and economic independence for the Ijaw community and Nigeria as a whole fueled his life's work.

Despite facing financial hardships in his early years, he refused to let circumstances define his future. His success was built on sheer determination, excellence, and an unshakable commitment to lifting others up—especially those often overlooked by society.
